Frédéric Chopin

Image Source

Frederic Francois Chopin, was born in born March 1, 1810 in what is now Poland. Died on October 17, 1849, in Paris, France. He was a Polish French composer and pianist during the romantic period. Well known for his piano solo pieces and concerti. He wrote little piano works and is known for his music’s tones. He is considered one of the greatest music tone poets because of his imagination and craftmanship. May of his piano works are brief.
